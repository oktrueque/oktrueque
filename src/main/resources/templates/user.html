My boyfriend and I decided to spend 10 days in Puglia for the summer holidays. We liked the idea of staying in a typical Apulean dry stone hut with a conical roof: a TRULLO. Trullo edera was clean, perfectly equipped, bright, specious with everything you could need for a relaxing holiday. The outdoor space is very lovely, we enjoyed having breakfasts and dinners surrounded by the Valle D'Itria typical landscape. Moreover there is a pretty good chimney barbecue, we enjoyed very much making our bbq with the tasty meat from local producers, in particular the "bombette". The trullo is in a great location close to very beautiful villages such as Cisternino, Alberobello, Ostuni and Martina Franca. However we had to drive 1 hour or 1 hour and an half to reach the most beautiful beaches of Puglia (Torre dell'Orso and Punta Prosciutto). Anna was a great host, her mother Tonia very kind and respectful person! We would love to come again and I would recommend it to my friends.
